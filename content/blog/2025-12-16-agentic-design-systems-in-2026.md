The Storybook team demonstrated their brand-new [Storybook MCP](https://storybook.js.org/addons/@storybook/addon-mcp) that helps teams generate UI by wielding their sturdy, well-considered design system component libraries. "Mouth Coding" and Collaborative Workflows In our work, we always _love_ using coded prototypes to short-circuit the circular, unproductive back-and-forth of design review meetings. That's why I feel that combining the generative power of AI with the well-considered structure of your design system is such a powerful combination. I paint a picture of it's possible to use DS+AI to create more collaborative workflows: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rVcOSnpvTc4  This unlocks the opportunity for brand-new kinds of collaborations between disciplines and teams. Non-technical team members can "mouth code" a product feature during a collaboration session and collectively we can see things come to life **_using the production-grade foundations of the design system_. ** **This is what distinguishes DS+AI from vibe coding; the AI is deliberately constrained to using the high-quality design system materials to ensure what's being generated adheres to the organization's established standards.** You can watch the whole video here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Vg78K3t9KYc  And if you're interested in learning how to wield design systems and AI together, we'd love it if you checked out our new course, [AI & Design Systems](https://aianddesign.systems/): [![](https://bradfrost.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/CleanShot-2025-11-23-at-22.13.51-1024x575.png)](https://aianddesign.systems/)
