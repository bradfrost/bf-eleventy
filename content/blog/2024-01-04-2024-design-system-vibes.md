**OUT:** Pattern Police   
**IN:** Empathic Sherpas **OUT:** An overstuffed design system library  
**IN:** A thoughtful, layer-cake [design system ecosystem](https://bigmedium.com/ideas/design-system-ecosystem.html)  **OUT:** Unnecessarily verbose documentation  
**IN:** Just enough, just-in-time documentation  **OUT:** Single discipline-focused design systems  
**IN:** Cross-disciplinary design systems that serve the entire org **OUT:** Rushing design system work to meet breakneck product deadlines  
**IN:**[Establishing pace layers](https://bigmedium.com/ideas/design-system-pace-layers-slow-fast.html) that allow product to move quickly and design systems to prioritize quality over speed.  **OUT:** Design systems as an off-to-the-side cost center  
**IN:** Design systems as critical UI infrastructure powering business-critical product initiatives **OUT:** Ad-hoc, lop-sided, and fragile releases  
**IN:** A thoughtful, transparent, and formal [governance process](https://bradfrost.com/blog/post/a-design-system-governance-process/) **OUT:** Protectiveness and ego  
**IN:** Curiosity, collaboration, partnership, and support
