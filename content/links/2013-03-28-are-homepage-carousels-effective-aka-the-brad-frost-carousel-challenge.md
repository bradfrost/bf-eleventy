The [University of York](http://www.york.ac.uk/) took the [Brad Frost Carousel Challenge](http://bradfrost.com/blog/post/carousels/) and released data about their homepage carousel interactions. They saw a large drop-off after the first panel, and discovered that removing panels actually increased engagement with the carousel area. They also explaining how to set up click tracking for carousels. There's a ton of great information in there so check it out!
